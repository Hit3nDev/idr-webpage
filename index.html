About IDR

Where Industry Meets Intelligence

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber, and technology risk. We operate at the intersection of academic excellence and real-world consequence, preparing practitioners for roles in regulated and high-consequence environments where failure is not an option.

Through a pioneering partnership with leading UK universities, IDR fuses cutting-edge research with live industry projects. Our graduates don't just understand risk. They shape, govern, and manage it at the frontier of financial services, critical infrastructure, and emerging technology.
